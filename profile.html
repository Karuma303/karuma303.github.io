Practical experience

In the 22 years of my freelance work as a software developer, I have implemented a large number of projects of various kinds for my own customers or on behalf of well-known digital agencies.

My activities have covered the entire spectrum of modern, agile software development - whether planning the architecture, implementation, testing or deployment. The software modules developed by me ran on a large number of different devices, on servers, on smartphones, in the web browser, on POS touch terminals and on large digital signage installations.

The focus of my activities in the last years was mainly on the development of mobile applications (Android & cross-platform), as well as in web development (TypeScript, VUE, Tauri etc.).

I can say with a clear conscience that new technologies do not scare me and that even when learning new tools or programming languages, I can usually work productively after a short time. My nature is quite open and sociable and I get along well with other people in a team.
